Guacamole Recipe

You will learn how to make this delicious dish!

This is also a very delicious dish! Complement the menu of your party with Guacamole whenever you make Chili! They match perfectly! It tastes amazing alone too, you should try!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Tomatoes.
  • 1 big Avocado.
  • 2 lemons.
  • Salt.
  • Coriander to taste.

Steps:

  1. Crumple the avocado in an big bowl with a fork.
  2. Add 3 pinches of salt and squeeze the lemons in the same bowl.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Cut the tomatoes in cubes and cut some coriander as well.
  5. Add everything together and mix well one more time.
