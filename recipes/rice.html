Rice Recipe

You will learn how to make this delicious dish!

This is a very common complement for Chili and Guacamole. It won't add much taste for the meal, but is very nice to have some, especially in a party.

Ingredients:

  • Rice.
  • Salt.
  • Tap Water.

Steps:

  1. In a big pan, put your rice and wash it 3 times or until you see the water is clean.
  2. Put enough water to cover all the rice in the pan.
  3. Add a pinch of salt and leave it in the stove at low heat until all the water evaporates.
  4. Cover the pan with its lid, and let it rest for 10 minutes.
